The Volcano Zone

The Volcano Zone helps you to track Volcanoes. Just enter a country's name and you can see if that country is safe from Volcanoes.

The Hurricane Zone

The Hurricane Zone helps you to track Volcanoes. Just select a state and a district and you can see if that district is safe from Volcanoes.
